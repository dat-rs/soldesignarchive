Eugen Aloisius Hirsch (1923-2001) was an Austrian graphic designer, illustrator, and artist that lived and worked in Brazil. He was the art director for Brazilian publisher <a class="text cat-link publisher" href="/publishers/Civilização Brasileira/">Civilização Brasileira</a>.
