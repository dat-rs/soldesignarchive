Jaime Martins Barata (1899-1970) was a Portuguese artist, illustrator, and stamp and coin designer. He started designing stamps for <a class="text cat-link publisher" href="/publishers/Correios de Portugal/">Correios de Portugal</a> in 1938 and, from 1947 until his retirement in 1969, he worked as artistic advisor to the company.
